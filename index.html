I am a PhD student at IIT Bombay guided by Prof. Abhay Karandikar. I am currently working on the topic of designing a Frugal 5G network architecture for providing affordable broadband access to rural areas. Earlier, I worked on the project of testing the feasibility of broadband access in rural areas through TV White Spaces. While working on this project, I worked as an intern at NICT, Japan where my focus was to develop an algorithm for inter-operator spectrum allocation.

I have completed my B.Tech. in Electronics and Communication Engineering from SVNIT, Surat. During my B.Tech., I got a two months internship opportunity at IIT Delhi.
